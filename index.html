📠 Musical Image Scanner

Press play, and music will be created from a random image.

How does it work?

The image is "split" into columns and each column is "split" into four rows. Each row has its color data averaged, and converted into a pitch. The result is a four-part harmony, inspired by a soprano, alto, tenor, bass chorale.

Random images from Unsplash. Made with Tone.js and p5.js
